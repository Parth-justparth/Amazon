How it works

A real, explainable AI pipeline — no black box.

1

Pick your item

Sign in and choose an item from your own orders. We check it's still within the return window.

2

Tell us the details

Pick a reason and what you'd like. Note what's still with the item — it affects resale value.

3

AI inspects photos

Upload photos from a few angles. OpenAI vision verifies it's the right product and grades condition 0–100.

4

AI recommends

Weighing condition, reason, value and logistics, it recommends Keep-It, Replace, Resale or Donate — and explains why.

5

You get rewarded

Your refund is processed, CO₂ saved is shown, and Green Points land in your wallet.
